What we build. What you deploy.

Every asset below is built programmatically from the terminal. You own everything.

Competitor intelligence, scraped live

Using Perplexity and Playwright MCPs, we scrape competitor sites, pull customer reviews, and map market gaps — all from the terminal. You get a research brief that would take a human analyst a full week.

Positioning and brand voice, built on proven frameworks

Expert instruction manuals (Skills) encode Schwartz awareness levels and Ogilvy's Big Idea method directly into the AI. We generate contrarian angles, unique mechanisms, and voice guidelines — tested against your actual market data.

Conversion-optimized pages that don't look like AI made them

Using our Front-end Design skill, we build landing pages that avoid AI tropes — no purple gradients, no rounded-corner SaaS templates. Instead: a confident, anti-corporate look with copy that converts. Deployed straight from the terminal.

Interactive tools, not boring ebooks

Instead of a PDF nobody reads, we build revenue calculators, self-assessment audits, and interactive tools directly into your site to capture emails. Built programmatically in the terminal, embedded on your pages.

Dozens of ad variants, generated in minutes

Programmatically create video ads via Remotion with custom fonts and brand colors — directly from the terminal. Use Glyph and Nano Banana Pro to generate dozens of static image variations for A/B testing. This is the testing machine: launch, measure, iterate at a pace that isn't humanly possible.
