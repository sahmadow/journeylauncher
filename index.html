Build a complete system
in one session.

Research

30–60 minutes. Gather market landscapes, competitor gaps, customer pain points. MCPs pull real-time data from search engines, competitor sites, and review platforms.

Foundation

Define brand voice. Find 3–5 positioning angles — Contrarian, Unique Mechanism, Big Idea. Skills apply Schwartz awareness levels and Ogilvy principles automatically.

Structure

Map keyword opportunities. Identify content quick wins for the first 90 days. Build the editorial calendar and content architecture from research data.

Assets

Build landing pages, email sequences, lead magnets — all conversion-optimized. Every asset references the research and foundation layers for consistency.

Iteration

Ruthlessly reject generic output. Enforce brand voice until expert standards are met. Every round of iteration feeds new context back into the system.
